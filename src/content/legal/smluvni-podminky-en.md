## Contractual Terms

**Last updated: October 2023**

These are the contractual terms for specific services provided by Progressivo.

### 1. Service Agreement

Specific terms regarding the scope of work, deliverables, and timelines will be defined in individual service agreements.

### 2. Fees and Payment

Fees for our services are set out in the service agreement or our current rate card.

### 3. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential all non-public information obtained during the course of the engagement.

### 4. Termination

Either party may terminate the agreement with written notice as specified in the contract.
